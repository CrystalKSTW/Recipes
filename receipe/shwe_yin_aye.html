Shwe Yin Aye

Shwe yin aye (Burmese: ရွှေရင်အေး) is a traditional Burmese dessert commonly associated with the Thingyan season

Ingredients

  • Coconut Milk
  • Coconut Jelly
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • White Bread
  • Shredded Coconut
  • Sticky Rice

How to make

In a bowl, combine small boiled sago, agar jelly, bread, sugar, coconut cream, sticky rice and ice cube.Easy Right?
